About Sony CSL - Paris

Sony CSL - Paris is a leading-edge organization that works to create new research areas and paradigms, as well as new technologies and businesses for the common social good.
Our researchers are working on multi-disciplinary and innovative projects and require complex technical approaches and tools that aim to tackle high level societal, technological and scientific challenges.
