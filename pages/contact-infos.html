    Sony CSL - Paris Research laboratories
    We are a small, bottom-up and people-driven research group. Most researchers have a background in computer science and combine their skills with their field of interest to tackle multi-disciplinary research questions. Working at CSL requires a healthy level of autonomy and a solid conviction to accomplish the proof-of-concepts and communicate the results. We try to maintain a dynamic research environment in which the researchers can give feedback and collaborate freely.
